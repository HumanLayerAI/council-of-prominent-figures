## What It Does

A live, interactive Claude artifact that simulates structured deliberation among historically and intellectually prominent figures. The system analyzes complex questions by forcing **explicit disagreement, assumption testing, and epistemic accountability** before producing a synthesized conclusion.

Rather than generating a single coherent response, the artifact orchestrates multiple constrained reasoning voices, each representing a distinct intellectual lens (law, psychology, strategy, ethics, technology), and resolves their conflict through a controlled synthesis layer.
